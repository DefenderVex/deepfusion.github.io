Born June 24th 1994 in Sydney Australia Oliver was the son of Christopher & Gracen Clark along with his twin brother Tyler. The two remained
inseparable from the day they were born, even more so after both their parents will killed in the car crash at the age of five. The two would
later be adopted by Alexander & Jasmine Drake a year later. The two would continue to reside in Australia until the age of fourteen when they both
decide to change their last names to Drake before moving to the United States with their adoptive parents in search of a fresh start.
After the four of them settled down in los Angeles California, Oliver & Tyler both took up surfing but over time Oliver became more interested in
motosports. On his sixteenth birthday Oliver's adoptive parents got him a dirt bike which marked the start of his interest on a profressional
level. Over the coming years he practiced and trained for national championships, by the age of twenty he was the winner of the AMA Motocross
Championship twice where he gained the reputation for being an incredibly gifted rider.
Oliver & Tyler would continue to remain incredibly close over the years, always finding time for family and always surfing together in the summer.
After The Cascade they were both forced to flee the coast but became split apart, Oliver continues to believe the two brothers will find their
way back to each other in the coming weeks but only time will tell.
